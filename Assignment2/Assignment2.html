Why Math and Computers at Spelman?

I know you all are wondering... What in the world was she thinking? Mathematics as a major and Computer Science as a minor to follow up. I know. I wouldn't have approved at all. Interesting enough, I started out as a Biology major; I thought I'd make the most money and save the world at the same time. You know, kill two birds with one stone I guess. I quickly found out that this major was not for me. Ironically, I was taking a "hard" math class but I was acing each test! That was enough motivation for me, so I switched over. Now we know parents do not typically approve of abrupt changes so I also wrote my dad a letter explaining how my life could still make sense with this change and of course I listed all of the highest paying jobs along with it. As for Computer Science, Dr. Marshall, the current chair of the department, kind of convinced me, so I went along with it and now I'm here and I've learned so much!
